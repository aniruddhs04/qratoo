Features

Client-Product Exclusivity

We recognise the individual identity of our client and how his or her requirements are distinctive as compared to the rest. Hence, it is expected that the portfolio of every client is different in terms of impression and presentation.

There are no fixed or pre-defined formats that we use to cater to our clients. Our team constructs the required product from scratch. Every detail that is acquired from the client is curated and presented in a way that it adds positively to the rest of the portfolio. Every service is unique to the client and to the specific application in order to leave a lasting impression on the recruiters.

Human Interface

Even the best algorithms or softwares cannot replicate the robustness and experience that the human element brings in. The entire process of writing and updation of a CV, cover letter, SOP or essay is done in such a fashion that it cannot be recreated by a machine. Customer satisfaction is our primary focus. Hence, our team of curators use all their experience and wisdom to bring to you a product that is dynamically altered till the user feels that the product is adequate to his or her needs.

We provide a human interface with the client through email correspondence and conferencing. This results in a rigorous dialogue and at the end of the process the client receives robust portfolio specific to his or her requirements.

Free Updation

We understand that a customer requires to continuously change his portfolio as his skill-set grows and as he undertakes new ventures. Hence, it is not enough to provide a one-time service.

All our services are available for the period of time as per the subscription plan. This also includes necessary updation, modification of the portfolio. With newly acquired information from the client, our team shall periodically update the CV and produce a tailor-made document along with the Cover Letter every time the client applies for a new venture.

Dashboard

The dashboard is a unique service that our team is currently working on and will soon be made available for our paid users and integrated to the website. We aim to give you the experience of an exclusive space that reflects your present academic and professional undertakings and achievements. The dashboard will be the one-stop destination for all your certifications and documentation to back the claims you make during the application. Such documents can later be linked or attached to your CV.

The dashboard will also contain a ‘virtual notebook’ which you may use as to-do list or memo for your current professional and academic ventures. Say, you can also schedule a course you are working on or you are interested in and keep it updated in the dashboard, so that we can add that to your CV and cover letter seamlessly, once the course is complete. Through regular email updates, we push you to update certifications for the said course. Hence, there is a hassle-free real-time maintenance of your career portfolio.
