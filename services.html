Personal Essay

Admission committees in undergraduate schools receive a large number of applications with similar SAT scores. However, whether the student is accepted or not depends on the personal essay (interchangeably : personal statement) submitted. The personal essay is usually looked at by the universities to acquire knowledge of the person who is applying for the university.

Though your marks in the preliminary exam play a major factor, presenting your dynamic achievements and participation in school projects and teams is a very important task. Your essay should be an insight into your personality and impress on the recruiters why you deserve to be accepted. These factors make you stand out among other applicants, as it provides an evidence of who you are beyond your skill-set and test scores. But loading your application with non-compulsory information can be unfavorable towards you.

In the next step, our counsellors set up a conference with the client to get to know the client and chalk out the final details. Through continuous correspondence with our team, create a personal statement that creates a genuine impression during your college application.
